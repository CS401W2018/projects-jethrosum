4. What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

OL stands for ordered lists and UL stands for unordered lists. OL produces a numbered list while UL produces a bulleted list.

Examples of an OL

  1. One
  2. Two
  3. Three
  1. One
  2. Two
  3. Three

Example of a UL

  • One
  • Two
  • Three
