Genshin Impact

A Beginner's Guide

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Adventure Rank, and how does it work?

Adventure Rank (AR) is the main progression system in Genshin Impact. Players increase their AR by earning Adventure EXP, which unlocks new challenges and areas.

2. How do I obtain Primogems in Genshin Impact?

Primogems can be obtained through daily commissions, quests, events, achievements, and exploring the game world.

3. What are elemental reactions, and how can I use them effectively?

Elemental reactions are triggered by combining elements, such as Pyro and Hydro for Vaporize. They amplify damage and can turn the tide of battles.

4. How do I build the best team composition?

Focus on synergy between characters' elemental abilities, weapon types, and artifacts. Include a mix of damage dealers, supports, and healers.
