Genshin Impact

Current Story

In the mystical world of Teyvat, the Traveler, a wanderer searching for their lost twin sibling, arrives in Mondstadt, the city of freedom. They soon discover that the region is under threat from a mysterious storm dragon, Dvalin, manipulated by a dark force called the Abyss Order. With the help of Paimon, their companion, and local allies like the Knights of Favonius and the Anemo Archon (god of wind), Barbatos, the Traveler frees Dvalin from the Abyss Order's control. This journey reveals the Traveler's unique ability to wield elemental powers and hints at a larger mystery involving the world’s gods, the Archons, and the Abyss Order.

The Traveler’s quest continues through other nations in Teyvat, each governed by a different Archon and their respective element. In the mountainous land of Liyue, ruled by the Geo Archon, Rex Lapis, they investigate his sudden “death” during a festival, uncovering a plot involving ancient contracts, human ambition, and divine intervention. In the region of Inazuma, a nation isolated by its Electro Archon in pursuit of "eternity," the Traveler becomes entangled in a civil conflict over the Archon’s Vision Hunt Decree, a policy stripping citizens of their elemental powers. With the help of rebels, they confront the Archon and help her rediscover her humanity, ending the oppressive decree and restoring balance to the nation.

As the Traveler journeys to Sumeru, the land of wisdom governed by the Dendro Archon, they confront a conspiracy involving the nation’s governing body and forbidden technology that endangers its people. Teaming up with scholars and desert-dwelling warriors, they expose and dismantle the scheme, helping the young Archon embrace her role as a protector. Along the way, the Traveler continues to encounter the Fatui, a powerful organization with mysterious motives, and the Abyss Order, deepening the mystery of their sibling’s allegiance with this dark faction. Their path next leads to Natlan, a fiery land of passion and conflict, where the Traveler prepares to uncover more truths about the Archons, Teyvat’s history, and their sibling’s enigmatic journey.

Rex Lapis values tradition and honor, ensuring that Liyue thrives both economically and culturally.

Under Kusanali’s leadership, Sumeru becomes a center for intellectual advancement and innovation.

Though dramatic and theatrical, Furina represents the ideals of fairness and creativity, shaping Fontaine's culture around water and balance.
