The system has a "pity" mechanic, ensuring players get a five-star reward after a certain number of wishes if they haven't already received one. For example, the soft pity for five-star characters starts around 75 wishes, with guaranteed five-star at 90. If a player doesn't get the featured five-star character, the next one is guaranteed to be featured, making it a mix of luck and gradual reward.
