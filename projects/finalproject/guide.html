Genshin Impact

A Step by Step Guide

Adventure Rank

Adventure Rank (AR) is the primary progression system in Genshin Impact. Players can raise their Adventure Ranks through Adventure EXP (Experience) gained through fighting over-world opponents, collecting chests, and completing events and quests.

World Level

World Level is the over-world progression system in Genshin Impact that affects enemy difficulty level, and the quality of items players can obtain by defeating bosses and enemies.

Adventure Handbook

The Adventure Handbook is the method by which players can view available quests, commissions, and domains, among other useful features.

Archon Quests

Archon Quests explores the main story of Genshin Impact. Every archon quest varies and builds upon the previous one. Archon quests also tend to offer the biggest rewards.

Tips and Tricks

  • Complete the Main Story (Archon Quests)
  • Unlock New Regions
  • Level Up Your Characters
  • Focus on Your Starter Character and Team
  • Complete Daily Commissions
  • Use Primogems Wisely
  • Upgrade Artifacts and Weapons
  • Explore the World and Gather Resources
  • Complete World Quests and Side Quests
  • Unlock Domains and Boss Fights
  • Learn about Elemental Reactions
  • Use your Resin Wisely
  • Join Co-Op Mode and Play with Friends
